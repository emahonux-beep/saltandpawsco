Our story

Meet the Team

Salt and Paws Co was founded with one simple belief: pets deserve care that feels like home.

What started as a love for animals and a desire to provide dependable, thoughtful pet care quickly grew into something more. We saw a need for personalized, in-home services that prioritize comfort, routine, and genuine connection—not crowded kennels or rushed visits.

As pet owners ourselves, we understand how hard it can be to leave your furry family behind. That's why we focus on creating a calm, consistent experience built on trust, communication, and care. Whether it's a walk, a drop-in visit, overnight care, or boarding, every pet is treated as if they were our own.

Salt and Paws Co is proudly family-run, and every service we offer is rooted in reliability, compassion, and attention to detail. From daily updates to personalized care plans, our goal is simple: to give you peace of mind and give your pet the love they deserve while you're away.

We're honored to be trusted with your pets and grateful to be part of their everyday lives.

Our Team

Erin Mahon

Owner

Hi, I'm Erin—the owner of Salt and Paws. I'm a lifelong animal lover with a passion for providing thoughtful, dependable care for pets of all kinds. By day, I work remotely as a product designer, which gives me the flexibility to be hands-on with the pets I care for and fully present during each visit.

I believe great pet care comes from consistency, patience, and truly getting to know each animal's personality and routine. Whether I'm stopping by for a visit, heading out for a walk, or staying overnight, my goal is always the same—to make sure every pet feels safe, comfortable, and cared for.

I'm proud to run Salt and Paws alongside my husband, Dan, and I'm grateful for the opportunity to care for such amazing pets and their families.

Daniel Hyland

Pet Sitter

Dan is the calm, steady presence behind Salt and Paws Co—what many clients affectionately call the pet whisperer. He has a natural way with animals, especially those who may be shy, nervous, or slow to warm up.

Pets tend to gravitate toward him, and he has a gift for building trust quickly through patience, consistency, and a gentle approach.

Whether it's a long walk, an overnight stay, or simply keeping a routine on track, Dan takes pride in making sure every pet feels comfortable and secure. His attentiveness and easygoing nature make him an invaluable part of the Salt and Paws team.

Together, Erin and Dan work as a team to provide thoughtful, dependable care—ensuring every pet feels right at home while their owners are away.
